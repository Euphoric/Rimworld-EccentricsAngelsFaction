[h1]About[/h1]
Adds [b]Eccentric's Angels[/b] faction.

[b]Features:[/b]
[list]
[*] Pawns use Eccentric's Tech items, apparrel and weapons
[*] All pawns are female ([i]HAR currently has bug that breaks this feature[/i])
[*] This faction will buy and sell Eccentric's Tech items
[*] Custom ideligon ([b]work in progress[/b])
Includes custom culture, structure and a meme. [i]These are meant to be for flavor and are not meant to be used by players.[/i]
[*] Custom backstories ([b]help wanted[/b])
[*] Some of the pawns have animal ears and tail cosmetic bionics ([b]not implemented[/b])
[/list]
